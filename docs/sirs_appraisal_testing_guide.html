Accessing the System

Start by opening either the live application or your local development server.

Live Environment https://appraisalsystem.site

For local testing, start the Django server with python manage.py runserver, then visit http://127.0.0.1:8000 in your browser.

Default Test Accounts

Use these accounts when the database has been seeded with test data. Otherwise, the HR Admin can create them.

Phase 1

HR Administration Setup

Role: HR / Admin admin

  1. Log in to the HR Portal.
    • Log in using the admin credentials.
    • Confirm that the HR Dashboard displays active cycles, staff counts, and recent activity.
  2. Manage Departments and Staff.
    • Go to Departments and verify that departments can be added or edited.
    • Go to Staff Directory and verify that staff details, department assignments, supervisors, and HODs can be managed.
  3. Manage Appraisal Cycles.
    • Open Appraisal Cycles.
    • Create a new cycle, such as 2025 Annual Review, or edit an existing one.
    • Inside the cycle, configure KPIs, competencies, and narrative questions.
    • Set the cycle status to Active so staff can begin appraisals.
  4. Verify setup.
    • Confirm that the cycle appears on the HR Dashboard as the active cycle.
Phase 2

The Appraisal Workflow

Test the complete flow from staff self-appraisal to supervisor review and HOD approval.

1

Staff Self-Appraisal

Staff completes and submits the appraisal form.

2

Supervisor Review

Supervisor scores, comments, and forwards the appraisal.

3

HOD Approval

HOD reviews, comments, and gives final approval.

Step 1: Staff Self-Appraisal

Role: Staff staff_tax

  1. Log in and check the dashboard.
    • Log in as staff_tax.
    • Confirm that the dashboard shows a pending appraisal for the active cycle.
  2. Complete the appraisal form.
    • Click Start Self-Appraisal.
    • Enter targets, achievements, and self scores from 1 to 5 for quantitative KPIs.
    • Grade competencies and add comments.
    • Complete the required narrative sections such as challenges and key achievements.
  3. Save and submit.
    • Test Save as Draft to ensure entries are saved without submission.
    • Click Submit for Review.
    • Confirm that the status changes to Submitted and the form is locked for editing.

Step 2: Supervisor Review

Role: Supervisor sup_tax

  1. Check dashboard and notifications.
    • Log in as sup_tax.
    • Check the notification bell for a new submission alert.
    • Confirm that the To Review dashboard counter shows a pending appraisal.
  2. Grade the appraisal.
    • Open Team Appraisals.
    • Click Review beside the staff member’s name.
    • Review the staff self-scores.
    • Enter supervisor scores from 1 to 5 for each KPI and competency.
    • Add supervisor comments and complete the final recommendation section.
  3. Submit to HOD.
    • Optionally test Return to Staff for Revision. If used, the staff member must resubmit.
    • Click Submit to HOD.
    • Confirm that the weighted final score is calculated and the appraisal is forwarded to the HOD.

Step 3: HOD Final Approval

Role: HOD hod_tax

  1. Review the pending appraisal.
    • Log in as hod_tax.
    • Check the notification bell.
    • Go to Department Appraisals.
    • Confirm that the appraisal is marked as Under Review.
  2. Approve the appraisal.
    • Click Review to view staff inputs and supervisor grades/comments.
    • Add an HOD comment at the bottom.
    • Click Approve Appraisal.
    • Confirm that the status changes to Approved and the final score is locked.
Phase 3

Reporting and Edge Cases

Confirm final visibility, reporting, exports, and admin recovery options.

  1. Verify completion as Staff.
    • Log back in as staff_tax.
    • Confirm that the dashboard shows the appraisal as Approved.
    • Open the finalized appraisal and confirm that it is read-only.
  2. Check HR reports and overrides.
    • Log in as admin.
    • Open Reports and verify system-wide analytics such as average scores and completion rates.
    • In Appraisal Cycles, test whether HR can remove appraisals for a restart or re-add staff who were mistakenly excluded.
  3. Test data export if configured.
    • From the Reports section, test Excel or Word export generation.
    • Confirm that exported files contain the correct appraisal data for payroll, promotion, or record-keeping use.

Completion Checklist

Use this quick checklist before marking the test cycle as complete.

  • HR can create and activate an appraisal cycle.
  • Staff can save a draft and submit a self-appraisal.
  • Supervisor can review, score, comment, return, or submit to HOD.
  • HOD can review and approve the final appraisal.
  • Final approved appraisal is locked and visible as read-only.
  • Reports and exports show accurate scores, statuses, and completion data.
End of Testing Guide
